What's running today & what's available

What's running today

These are live in your hub right now — everyone with access can use them.

Inventory Management
Multi-location tracking with bin locations and real-time counts.
Quotes & Templates
Build quotes from reusable line-item bundles. Discount %/$, surplus pickup, and split-line cost layers.
Build Tracker
Material allocation, usage recording, surplus capture on close-out.
Surplus Management
Track leftover materials from prior builds for reuse on future jobs at original cost.
Purchase Orders
Create POs, receive partial or full quantities, audit-trailed inventory updates per location.
Invoices
Full line-item invoices with status flow (draft → sent → viewed → paid). Build → invoice in one click.
Transfers
Move inventory between warehouse, stores, and active build sites.
Physical Counts
Structured cycle-count workflow with variance tracking against expected on-hand.
Auto Reorder Suggestions
Reorder points calculated from usage history; one-click apply across the catalog.
PO Auto-Generation
Build POs automatically for every item below its reorder point, grouped by preferred vendor.
Accounts Receivable / Payable
Dashboard cards plus dedicated reports with aging buckets.
Reports
Inventory value, low stock, build variance, surplus aging, inventory by location, and reorder suggestions.
Roles & Permissions
Admin, office, store, and foreman roles — the right people see the right things.
CSV Import
One-time bulk import for migrating from existing inventory systems.
How it fits together. A quote becomes a build. A build allocates and consumes inventory, then captures surplus on close-out. Surplus rolls back into the next quote at original cost. Closed builds turn into invoices in one click. Receiving against a PO updates inventory at the chosen location automatically. Every stock change goes through the same audited adjustment path.

Premium features available

Optional capabilities that can be added to your system — not on the roadmap by default, but ready to build when you're ready. Each is scoped and quoted separately.

Operations
Barcode / QR Scanning Premium
Scan items with your phone camera for instant lookup, stock adjustments, and physical counts. Generate barcodes for bin locations.
Delivery / Dispatch Tracker Premium
Track deliveries to job sites: what's being sent, who's driving, when it left, estimated arrival. Foremen know what's coming.
Equipment / Tool Checkout Premium
Track company tools and equipment checked out to crews. Know where everything is.
Workforce
Time Tracking Premium
Crews clock in/out from their phones, tagged to a build or job site. Hours roll into job costing automatically and feed payroll-ready timesheets.
Sales Channels
Website Sales Channel Premium
Sync inventory and orders with your e-commerce site (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.). Online orders auto-deduct stock and flow into the hub as invoices.
Customer Experience & Service
Customer Portal Premium
Unique link for customers to view quotes, approve them, and check invoice status. No login required.
Warranty Tracking Premium
Track warranties on items sold to customers. Get notified before warranties expire.
Financial & Cost
Job Costing Premium
Full job cost breakdown: materials, labor, equipment, subcontractors. True profitability per build.
Vendor Price Comparison Premium
Track pricing from multiple vendors per item over time. See who has the best price when creating POs.
Customer Credit Tracking Premium
Credit limits, payment history, and slow-payer flags. Know who to extend credit to.
Productivity, Visibility & Documentation
Notifications & Alerts Premium
Email/push notifications for low stock, PO received, invoice viewed, build close-out, transfer arriving.
Activity Feed Premium
Live feed of everything happening across the system in real time.
Photo Attachments Premium
Attach photos to builds, surplus items, damage reports, and inventory items.
Seasonal Demand Forecasting Premium
Predict demand spikes from historical usage. Spring fencing, fall harvest prep — plan ahead.
