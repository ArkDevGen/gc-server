Square Retail tiers

Square charges per store location. The warehouse does not need a Square subscription — it has no register, and inventory there lives in the Hub.

Free

$0
per location / month
  • Basic POS & payments
  • Auto SKU creation
  • Discounts & refunds
  • Basic sales reporting
  • No advanced inventory or vendor management

Plus

$49
per location / month
  • Everything in Free
  • Inventory management & stock intake
  • Vendor & purchase order management
  • Cost of goods sold & inventory reports
  • Lower in-person processing rate
  • 30-day free trial

Premium

$149
per location / month
  • Everything in Plus
  • Cross-location returns & fulfilment
  • Transfer orders
  • 24/7 phone support
  • Lowest in-person processing rate
Recommended: Plus Plus is the right fit for Goodman Classic today and for where the business is heading. It's the lowest tier that includes real inventory management and vendor / PO data — both essential so Square stays in lockstep with the Hub at the registers. Premium's headline upgrades (cross-location returns, transfer orders) are already handled inside the Inventory Hub, so paying $100 more per location each month would duplicate work the Hub does. Free is too thin for a 2-store retail operation. 2 stores × $49 = $98/month.

Software prices shown are the published Square Retail subscription fees as of the date this document was generated. Square also charges per-transaction processing fees on card payments (paid only when a sale runs); these vary by tier and are not part of the monthly subscription cost above. Current Lightspeed and QuoteMachine figures are pulled from Goodman Classic's current invoices.
